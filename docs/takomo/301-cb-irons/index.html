About the Takomo 301 CB

The Iron 301 CB is Takomo's players cavity back, one-piece forged from S20C carbon steel and sharing its head weights, lofts, lies and offsets with the 301 MB muscle back. Compared with the MB it has a lower centre of gravity, perimeter weighting and a slightly wider sole, while keeping a short blade length and minimal offset. Lofts are traditional for a modern players iron, with a 34-degree 7-iron and a 47-degree pitching wedge, in a seven-club 4-iron through pitching wedge set. Takomo also sells a 301 Combo set pairing 301 CB long irons with 301 MB short irons. Offsets below are converted from Takomo's published millimetre figures.

Frequently asked questions

What is the loft of a Takomo 301 CB 7-iron?

The Takomo 301 CB 7-iron has a loft of 34 degrees, with a 62.5-degree lie and a 37-inch steel-shaft length.

What is the difference between the Takomo 301 CB and 301 MB?

They share identical lofts, lies, head weights and offsets. The CB is a cavity back with perimeter weighting, a lower centre of gravity and a slightly wider sole; the MB is a muscle-back blade.

What clubs come in a Takomo 301 CB set?

Seven clubs, 4-iron through pitching wedge. The pitching wedge is 47 degrees.

Finding a used set

The Takomo 301 CB is no longer in production, so the used market is the only source. Search eBay, 2nd Swing, Golf Avenue and the PGA Tour Superstore trade-in listings, and check the loft and lie against the chart above before buying — sets this age have often been bent from standard.
