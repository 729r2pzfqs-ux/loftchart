About the Takomo 101T

The Iron 101T is the compact version of Takomo's debut 101 iron, launched in mid-2022 as the brand's players-distance model. It keeps the hollow-body construction of the 101 but pairs a 431 stainless body with a 450 stainless face in a head with a thinner topline, shorter blade length and roughly a millimetre less offset. Lofts are two degrees weaker than the 101 through most of the set, with a 32-degree 7-iron and a 46-degree pitching wedge, and the stock set is 4-iron through pitching wedge. Offsets below are converted from Takomo's published millimetre figures. An archived manufacturer page from June 2022, within days of launch, carries the same lofts, lies and offsets Takomo publishes today, so this table describes the club as originally sold as well as in its current listing. Like the 101, the 101T was a cast hollow-body stainless iron from the start rather than a forged S20C blade.

Frequently asked questions

What is the loft of a Takomo 101T 7-iron?

The Takomo 101T 7-iron has a loft of 32 degrees, with a 62.5-degree lie and 2.4 mm of offset.

What is the difference between the Takomo 101 and the 101T?

The 101T has a thinner topline, shorter blade length and about 1 mm less offset, and its lofts are two degrees weaker through most of the set - 32 degrees at the 7-iron versus 30 degrees for the 101.

What clubs come in a Takomo 101T set?

Seven clubs, 4-iron through pitching wedge, with a 46-degree pitching wedge.

Is the Takomo 101T forged?

No. The 101T is a cast hollow-body iron with a 431 stainless steel body, as Takomo's own 2022 launch page described it. The forged S20C models in the range are the 201 and the 301 CB/MB.

Where it sits in the lineup

Introduced in 2022, the Takomo 101T is a current players distance set in the Takomo range, with no replacement announced. It replaced the Takomo 101. New sets are built to the standard lofts, lies and lengths in the chart above, though a fitting can move any of them — so confirm the numbers on the order sheet. Used sets appear on eBay, 2nd Swing and Golf Avenue, and should be measured against this chart before buying.
