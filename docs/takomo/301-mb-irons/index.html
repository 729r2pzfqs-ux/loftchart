About the Takomo 301 MB

The Iron 301 MB is Takomo's muscle-back blade, one-piece forged from soft S20C carbon steel with precision milling across the back of the head. It carries the same head weights, lofts, lies and offsets as the 301 CB cavity back, in a shorter blade with minimal offset and traditional muscle-back weight distribution. The 7-iron is 34 degrees and the pitching wedge 47 degrees, and the stock set is seven clubs, 4-iron through pitching wedge, with KBS Tour shafts and Lamkin Crossline grips. Takomo aims it at low single-figure and scratch players. Offsets below are converted from Takomo's published millimetre figures.

Frequently asked questions

What is the loft of a Takomo 301 MB 7-iron?

The Takomo 301 MB 7-iron has a loft of 34 degrees, with a 62.5-degree lie and a 37-inch steel-shaft length.

Are the Takomo 301 MB irons forged?

Yes. The 301 MB is a one-piece forging in soft S20C carbon steel, with the back of the head precision-milled after forging.

What clubs come in a Takomo 301 MB set?

Seven clubs, 4-iron through pitching wedge, with a 47-degree pitching wedge. Takomo also sells a 301 Combo set with 301 CB long irons and 301 MB short irons.

Finding a used set

The Takomo 301 MB is no longer in production, so the used market is the only source. Search eBay, 2nd Swing, Golf Avenue and the PGA Tour Superstore trade-in listings, and check the loft and lie against the chart above before buying — sets this age have often been bent from standard.
