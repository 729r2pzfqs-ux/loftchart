About the TaylorMade Stealth HD

Stealth HD was a line extension alongside the 2022 Stealth iron rather than a replacement for it, aimed at slower swing speeds and at players who miss to the right. It uses the same Cap Back design and ECHO Damping System but adds a draw bias, a very low centre of gravity that shifts progressively higher through the set, and much wider soles. Bounce is far higher than on the standard Stealth, from 11 degrees in the 5-iron to 16 degrees in the sand and lob wedges, and the lie angles are half a degree to one degree more upright. The 7-iron is 30 degrees, two degrees weaker than the standard Stealth. Specs below are the men's standard steel-shaft build.

Frequently asked questions

What is the loft of a TaylorMade Stealth HD 7-iron?

The TaylorMade Stealth HD 7-iron has a loft of 30 degrees, with a 63.5-degree lie and a 37.25-inch steel-shaft length.

How is the Stealth HD different from the standard Stealth iron?

Stealth HD is a draw-biased, higher-launching companion model rather than a replacement. Its lofts are about two degrees weaker (30 degrees at the 7-iron against 28), its lie angles are half a degree to one degree more upright, and its bounce is roughly twice as high across the set.

Does the Stealth HD iron set include a 4-iron?

No. The Stealth HD set starts at the 5-iron, which is 23.5 degrees, and runs through to a 59-degree lob wedge. TaylorMade expected buyers to fill the long-iron gap with hybrids or fairway woods.

What is the pitching wedge loft on the Stealth HD?

The Stealth HD pitching wedge is 44.5 degrees, a degree and a half weaker than the standard Stealth. It is followed by a 49-degree approach wedge, a 54-degree sand wedge and a 59-degree lob wedge.

Finding a used set

The TaylorMade Stealth HD ran from 2022 to 2023 and is no longer in production, so the used market is the only source. Search eBay, 2nd Swing, Golf Avenue and the PGA Tour Superstore trade-in listings, and check the loft and lie against the chart above before buying — sets this age have often been bent from standard.
