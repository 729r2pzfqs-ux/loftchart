About the Adams Idea a4

Adams marketed this set as the Idea Tech a4, the better-player half of the 2008 a4 generation that also produced the far more forgiving Idea Tech a4OS. It is a hybrid-iron combo set: the stock eight-club make-up is a 3-hybrid and 4-hybrid in place of the long irons, then six forged 8620 carbon steel irons from the 5-iron to the pitching wedge, with a quad cavity back, a forged stabiliser bar behind the face and a four-way cambered sole. Lofts run 26 degrees at the 5-iron to 45 degrees at the pitching wedge, with a 33-degree 7-iron, and matching GW, SW and LW were offered to extend the set. Only the iron clubs are listed below; the stock 3-hybrid (19 degrees, 59.25-degree lie, 40.5 inches) and 4-hybrid (22 degrees, 60-degree lie, 39.25 inches) are hybrids built around Adams' Boxer Technology rather than irons. Swing weights below are the steel-shaft figures - the same heads build to D1 with the stock graphite shafts.

Frequently asked questions

What is the loft of an Adams Idea a4 7-iron?

The Adams Idea a4 7-iron has a loft of 33 degrees, with a 62-degree lie and a 37.25-inch steel-shaft length.

What clubs come in a stock Adams Idea a4 set?

Eight clubs. A 3-hybrid and a 4-hybrid replace the long irons, followed by forged 5-iron through pitching wedge. Matching 50-degree GW, 55-degree SW and 60-degree LW heads were sold to extend the set.

Is the Adams Idea a4 the same club as the Idea Tech a4?

Yes. Adams sold it as the Idea Tech a4. It should not be confused with the Idea Tech a4OS, which is a much more forgiving cast set built around seven hybrids rather than two hybrids and six forged irons.

Are Adams Idea a4 irons forged?

The irons are, from 8620 carbon steel with a quad cavity back and a forged stabiliser bar behind the impact area. The 3-hybrid and 4-hybrid that come with the set are not forged irons.

Finding a used set

The Adams Idea a4 is no longer in production, so the used market is the only source. Search eBay, 2nd Swing, Golf Avenue and the PGA Tour Superstore trade-in listings, and check the loft and lie against the chart above before buying — sets this age have often been bent from standard.
