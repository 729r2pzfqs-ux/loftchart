About the Adams Idea Tech V4

The Idea Tech V4 is a hollow-bodied hybrid-iron set built around Adams' Velocity Slot Technology, with a Cut-Thru slot through the sole to raise face flex. The stock combo make-up is a 3-, 4- and 5-hybrid followed by 6-iron through pitching wedge, with a gap wedge available to extend the set; every head in the range shares the same hollow hybrid-iron construction, so Adams marketed even the short irons as hybrid irons. Lofts run in a strict 4-degree ladder with a 32-degree 7-iron and a 44-degree pitching wedge, and lie and length step evenly by 0.75 degrees and 0.75 inches per club. Only the iron-shaped clubs are listed below; the stock 3H (19 degrees), 4H (22 degrees) and 5H (25 degrees) are hybrids. The stock steel shaft was the lightweight True Temper GS75.

Frequently asked questions

What is the loft of an Adams Idea Tech V4 7-iron?

The Adams Idea Tech V4 7-iron has a loft of 32 degrees, with a 62.25-degree lie and a 37.5-inch length.

What clubs come in a stock Adams Idea Tech V4 set?

The stock combo set is a 3-hybrid, 4-hybrid and 5-hybrid replacing the long irons, then 6-iron through pitching wedge, with a 49-degree gap wedge offered separately.

Are the Adams Idea Tech V4 short irons hollow?

Yes. The whole range uses the same hollow hybrid-iron body with a Cut-Thru sole slot, which is why Adams labelled even the 8-iron, 9-iron and wedges as hybrid irons.

Finding a used set

The Adams Idea Tech V4 ran from 2011 to 2013 and is no longer in production, so the used market is the only source. Search eBay, 2nd Swing, Golf Avenue and the PGA Tour Superstore trade-in listings, and check the loft and lie against the chart above before buying — sets this age have often been bent from standard.
