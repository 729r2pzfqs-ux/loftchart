About the Ping Blueprint T

The Blueprint T is the muscle-back half of the pair that replaced the original Blueprint, launched alongside the cavity-back Blueprint S. It is a one-piece 8620 carbon steel forging with a thin top line, a compact profile and very little offset — 0.06 inches at the 7-iron, dropping to 0.03 at the pitching wedge, a hundredth more than the original Blueprint at each of those clubs. Lofts are a degree stronger than the original Blueprint at the 7-iron, 33 against 34, and every 3 to 9 iron is a quarter of an inch shorter than the matching Blueprint S to promote a lower flight. The set runs 3-iron through pitching wedge. Ping's colour-code (dot) system sets lie angle relative to standard: gold 4 flat, brown 3 flat, orange 2 flat, red 1 flat, BLACK standard, blue 1 upright, green 2 upright, white 3 upright, silver 4 upright, maroon 5 upright. The lies below are the BLACK (standard) lies.

Frequently asked questions

What is the loft of a Ping Blueprint T 7-iron?

The Ping Blueprint T 7-iron has a loft of 33 degrees, a standard (black dot) lie of 62.0 degrees and a steel-shaft length of 36.75 inches.

When were Ping Blueprint T irons made?

The Blueprint T was revealed in July 2023 and reached retail in January 2024, replacing the original Blueprint alongside the Blueprint S.

What is the difference between the Ping Blueprint T and Blueprint S?

They share lofts and lies club for club. The T is a one-piece muscle-back forging, the S a forged cavity back; the T's 3 to 9 irons are a quarter of an inch shorter for a lower flight, and the T carries slightly less offset in the long irons — 0.14 inches at the 3-iron against 0.17.

How much offset does the Ping Blueprint T have?

Very little: 0.14 inches at the 3-iron, 0.06 at the 7-iron and 0.03 at the pitching wedge.

Finding a used set

The Ping Blueprint T is no longer in production, so the used market is the only source. Search eBay, 2nd Swing, Golf Avenue and the PGA Tour Superstore trade-in listings, and check the loft and lie against the chart above before buying — sets this age have often been bent from standard.
